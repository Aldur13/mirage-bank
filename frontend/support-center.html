Frequently asked questions

Click Open Account at the top of any page. Fill in your name, email, password, and choose your account type (Personal, Youth, or Business). You'll be signed in immediately.
From your dashboard, click the Transfer button. Enter the recipient's account number (starting with MB-) and the amount. Transfers are instant and free within Mirage Bank.
A Youth account is a standard account with an added guardian link. The guardian can view transaction history and freeze or unfreeze the account. This is ideal for parents who want oversight of their child's finances while teaching financial independence.
As the account owner, go to your dashboard and use the Business Members section to invite colleagues by their Mirage Bank email address. Assign them a role (Manager or Employee) when inviting.
If your account is frozen by a guardian (Youth) or admin, you won't be able to make transfers or withdrawals. Contact your guardian or open a support ticket so our team can assist.
